About Us

RescuEase is a non-profit organization that helps people affected by extreme weather such as hurricanes, wildfires , and floods. Our team is dedicated to providing assistance and support to individuals and families in need during these difficult times.

We work closely with local emergency services, government agencies, and other non-profit organizations to ensure that our resources are used efficiently and effectively to help those who need it most.
